SOCIETY MEDIA PHILOSOPHY
The Bird Revelation and The Capitalism Machine

Dave Chappelle recites a story from Pimp during his latest standup special called The Bird Revelation. He ties the concepts of the story to capitalism, and mentions that it is an allegory for his leave from comedy.

FILM MEDIA PHILOSOPHY
Phantom Thread: Love and Desire

Phantom Thread is a film about the relationship between Reynolds Woodcock, an English couture dressmaker from the 1950's, and Alma, his most recent romantic interest. The film begins with the world that Reynolds inhabits- one where he is the center of attention of many women, and where his attention is solely focused on his work.

BOOKS MEDIA WRITING PHILOSOPHY
Modern Relationships and Murakami's Sputnik Sweetheart

In Murakami's Sputnik Sweetheart, every character is a satellite- a small, metal object, hurling through the emptiness of space. The narrator, who is in love with Sumire, watches as she falls in love with an older woman named Miu, who she refers to as her 'Sputnik Sweetheart'. They are all intimate with one another in different ways, but between each of them, an uncrossible distance remains.

FILM MEDIA PHILOSOPHY
Columbus: What happens when you meet your reflection?

Columbus is a film written and directed by Kogonada, and follows Jin, a Korean man with a dying father, and Casey, a 19-year old who forewent college to take care of her mother.

BOOKS MEDIA PHILOSOPHY
Call Me By Your Name - The Beauty of Vulnerability

Call Me By Your Name, written by André Aciman, is a novel about two boys- Oliver and Elio- who fall in love in the northern Italian countryside during the 80's. It is now a major motion picture.

FILM MEDIA PHILOSOPHY
Existential Loneliness in L'Avenir

L'avenir, or Things To Come, is a drama written and directed by Mia Hansen-Løve. It follows a philosophy teacher during a period of self-rediscovery after her husband announces that he is leaving her for another woman.
